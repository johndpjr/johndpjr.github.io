Areas of Interest

Being that bridge between the hardware and the software is becoming an increasingly important space in the world of computers, especially as hardware becomes more advanced and the software demands get larger. I enjoy low level enough to be interfacing with the OS (or being the OS!) and high level enough so that I can interface with existing libraries (and implement my own!).
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI): Take a look at my A.I. page if you're not convinced that AI is a force to be reckoned with! Please check it out anyway- it's really cool what ChatGPT can do nowadays! But seriously, AI has always been a topic that has piqued my curiousity and challenged me to think about the implications of such technologies and our role as software engineers to inform the public in an objective and truthful manner.

I've always been up to a good challenge, however. That's why one of my goals in the next few years is create a toy C compiler that can handle most vanilla C code. Please root for me!
